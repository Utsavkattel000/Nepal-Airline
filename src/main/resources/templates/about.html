Introduction:

Welcome to the Airline Ticket Booking System (ATBS), a user-friendly platform designed to make flight booking simple and hassle-free. ATBS was created as an academic project with a focus on privacy and ease of use. Our goal is to provide an intuitive booking experience without compromising your personal information or bombarding you with ads.

Privacy Policy:

At ATBS, we value your privacy. We don't collect any personal information from you, nor do we use cookies or engage in data collection. Our platform is completely ad-free, ensuring that your browsing experience remains smooth and uninterrupted.

When you book a flight with ATBS, we only ask for the essential information required for travel purposes. This includes your name, contact details, etc. Rest assured, this information is securely stored in our database and is never used for anything other than booking your flight.

While we take your privacy seriously, there are certain legal things that we must comply with. In rare cases such as flight accidents or legal proceedings, we may be required to provide passenger data to government authorities. However, we will only disclose this information when legally obligated to do so.

Please note that our current system does not support cancellations or refunds for booked flight tickets. While we may consider adding this feature in the future, there are no immediate plans to do so.

By using ATBS, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy. We are committed to protecting your data and providing you with a secure and straightforward booking experience.

Thank you for choosing ATBS for your travel needs. We appreciate your trust and look forward to serving you in the future.

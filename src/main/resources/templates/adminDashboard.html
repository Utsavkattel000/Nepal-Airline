Welcome to Your Dashboard, Admin!

Before making any changes, please read through these instructions carefully to ensure a smooth navigation experience. If you encounter any confusion, feel free to reach out to a developer for assistance.

1.Refreshing Your Dashboard: You can refresh your dashboard at any time by clicking on the logo located on the left side of the navbar.

2. Flight History: To view the flights you have flown in the past, simply click on the "Flight History" button in the navbar.

3. Pending Flights: Check your pending flights, those booked but yet to depart, by clicking on the "Pending Flights" button in the navbar.

4. Flown Flights Database: Access all flown flights ever recorded in the database, including passenger information, by clicking on the "Flown Flights" button in the navbar.

5. Adding Public Flights: Click on the "Add Public Flight" button to add a flight for a specific day in the future. Remember, this flight is for one day only and is not scheduled automatically by the system. (You can Scroll here.)

6. Adding New Scheduled Flights: Use the "Add New Flight" button to add a flight that will be automatically scheduled by the system for everyday use. Be cautious, as the system will schedule this flight instantly for today up to 12 days ahead. Contact a developer if you are confused about this.

7. Viewing System Flights: By clicking "View All Flights", you can see all flights that the system uses as a data source to schedule future flights. These flights are added using the "Add New Flight" button.

8. Booking Flights: Easily book flights of your choice by utilizing the search feature located on the left of this instruction box.

Remember, these instructions must be read thoroughly before making any changes. Should you encounter any confusion or require further assistance, don't hesitate to contact a developer. Happy navigating!
